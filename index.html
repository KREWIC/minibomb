Minibomb!

This website is a collection of programs that are too small to be featured as full projects. Feel free to browse and use any of them you like, or sign up for updates on new programs!

Open Projects

Spending Plan

This will help you find out what you can spend every week in order to reach a savings goal within a certain period of time.

Big Purchase Plan

This is for when you want to make a big purchase on your spending plan. Like if you want a PS5 or to go on a trip. (not a great option if you want to buy a car or a house or whatever)

Calorie Counter

This is an extremely simple calorie counter. Designed to be used throughout the day when you sit down at your computer.

Workout Planner

This is a workout planner. Select the muscle groups you want to workout and the program will supply you with a random assortment of workouts.
